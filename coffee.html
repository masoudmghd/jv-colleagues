Coffee Recipe

Time: 2-3 minutes
Difficulty: Easy
Yield: 1 cup (250ml)

Method

  • In this method, we will use inverted method.

Ingredients

  • 21g medium-fine ground coffee
  • 250ml hot water (90°C/194°F)
  • 1 paper filters

Instructions

  1. Preparation

    Rinse the paper filters with hot water and place them in the Aeropress cap.

    Place Aeropress in inverted position. You can push the plunger into the middle of the 4.

  2. Grind Coffee Beans

    Grind the coffee beans to a medium-fine powder.

  3. Add Coffee

    Place the Aeropress on the scale and add your coffee grinds.

  4. Add Water

    • Pour 50ml of hot water (90°C/194°F) over the coffee grounds.

      Stir gently to ensure all grounds are wet.

    • Wait for 30 seconds.

    • Pour another 100ml of hot water over the coffee grounds.

    • Wait for 30 seconds.

    • Pour another 100ml of hot water over the coffee grounds.

    • Stir gently to ensure all grounds are wet.

  5. Steep

    Let the coffee steep for 30 seconds.

  6. Press

    Put the cup on the Aeropress and flip it over gently.

    Gently press the plunger down over 15 seconds until you hear a hissing sound.

Tips

  • Use a digital scale to measure your coffee and water for consistency
  • Clean your Aeropress immediately after use
