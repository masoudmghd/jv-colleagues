Tea Recipe

Time: 7-8 minutes
Difficulty: Easy
Yield: 1 cup (250ml)

Characters

  • Your pack contains 7 grams of Exotic Foomanat Full Tea Leaf.
  • This tea is single origin and from a single farm.
  • This tea is from Fooman, Ghaleroodkhan.
  • This tea farm is located at an altitude of 200 meters above sea level and is of the foothill type.
  • This tea has a delicate, astringent taste that is usually felt without much bitterness. The flavor of the leaves can also be sensed.

Method

  • In this method, we will use inverted method.

Ingredients

  • 7 gr of Full Leaf Tea of Foomanat
  • 250ml hot water (94°C / 201°F)
  • 1 paper filters

Instructions

  1. Preparation

    Place Aeropress in inverted position. You can push the plunger into the middle of the 4.

  2. Add Tea

    Place the Aeropress on the scale and add your tea leaves.

  3. Steeping

    Pour 250ml of hot water over the tea and close the cap and filter and let it steep for the 7-8 minutes.

  4. Finishing

    Place the cup on the Aeropress and flip it over.

    Gently press the plunger down over 15 seconds until you hear a hissing sound.

Tips

  • Always use fresh, cold water for the best taste
  • Don't over-steep your tea as it can become bitter
  • Store tea in an airtight container away from moisture
  • Experiment with different steeping times to find your preferred strength
  • Consider using a timer to ensure consistent brewing
